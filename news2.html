BRICS Summit 2024: Detailed Discussions

The 2024 BRICS Summit concluded with significant agreements and initiatives aimed at promoting economic growth and geopolitical stability among member nations.

Major Agreements Reached

  • Trade Enhancement: A commitment to increase trade among BRICS countries by 30% by 2029.
  • Joint Research Initiatives: Establishing a BRICS research network to address common challenges in health and technology.
  • Climate Action: Collaborative efforts to combat climate change, including a pledge to reduce carbon emissions collectively.

Workshops and Panel Discussions

Throughout the summit, several workshops and panel discussions were held, focusing on:

Workshop Topic Facilitator Key Points
Digital Transformation Minister of Digital Affairs, India Harnessing technology for economic growth.
Sustainable Development Goals UN Representative Aligning BRICS initiatives with global SDGs.
Energy Security Minister of Energy, Russia Strategies for renewable energy collaboration.

Next Steps

As BRICS moves forward, member nations have agreed to hold quarterly meetings to review the progress of the initiatives launched during the summit.
