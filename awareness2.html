Cancer Prevention and Treatment

Understanding cancer prevention and treatment is essential for improving health outcomes. This page provides valuable information on how to reduce cancer risk and available treatment options.

Prevention Strategies

Prevention is the best approach to combat cancer.

  • Avoid tobacco: Quitting smoking and avoiding secondhand smoke can significantly reduce cancer risk.
  • Healthy diet: Consuming a diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains helps lower the risk.
  • Physical activity: Engaging in regular exercise can maintain a healthy weight and reduce cancer risk.
  • Sun protection: Use sunscreen and wear protective clothing to prevent skin cancer.
  • Vaccinations: Get vaccinated against viruses that can cause cancer, such as HPV and hepatitis B.
